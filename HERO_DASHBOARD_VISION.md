## The Score Truth (Trust Differentiator)

CreditReclaim is the only platform that tells users the full truth about credit scores. Most users believe they have "a credit score." They don't.

### What users need to understand:
- FICO sells multiple versions: FICO 8, 9, 10, 10T, plus industry-specific (Auto, Bankcard, Mortgage)
- Each of the three bureaus (Equifax, Experian, TransUnion) calculates these differently using its own data
- VantageScore is a separate model with its own versions (3.0, 4.0, 4plus)
- Result: a single person can have 20+ different "scores" at any moment
- The score Credit Karma shows is VantageScore 3.0 — NOT what most lenders use
- Auto lenders pull FICO Auto Score. Mortgage lenders pull older FICO Mortgage versions. Credit cards pull FICO Bankcard Score 8.

### Where this shows up in the product:
- **Dashboard score widget**: "What score is this?" tooltip explains which model and why it may differ from other apps
- **Training Module 1**: Leads with score literacy before any dispute education. Sets the platform's tone — we tell the truth others won't.
- **Marketing**: "The score Credit Karma shows you isn't the score your mortgage lender will see." Cold-traffic hook that names real user frustration.

### Why it matters strategically:
- Builds trust faster than any competitor (Lexington Law, Sky Blue do not teach this)
- Defuses user disappointment when their dashboard score differs from other apps
- Creates platform credibility moat — once users learn this from us, they trust everything else we say
- Frames CreditReclaim as the educator, not just another tool

### Language guardrails:
- Say: "The score you see may not be the score your lender uses"
- Don't say: "FICO and the bureaus manipulate numbers in favor of lenders" (defamation risk against litigious entities)
- The neutral framing is equally damning and legally safe
