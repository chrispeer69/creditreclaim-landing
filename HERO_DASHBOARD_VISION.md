# HERO'S DASHBOARD OUTLINE

## What it feels like:
Not clinical data. Not a spreadsheet. A **narrative of your life improving.**

## Top Section - The Win:
- Your credit score (big, prominent, updated real-time)
- Score improvement graph (visual proof you're winning)
- "Items removed this month" (celebration)
- "Disputes resolved" (countdown to 7-year clean slate)

## Middle Section - Your Arsenal:
- Active disputes (but not as a list - as battles)
  - Creditor | Status | **Days Until Forced Response** | Your Move
  - Shows you're DOING something, not waiting
  - Educational callout: "Why 30 days matters" (link to training module)
- Letters you can send TODAY (not all 100, just the 3-5 that match your situation)
  - One-click send or download
  - "Why send this now?" explanation right there
- Responses received (celebration moment)
  - "Bureau confirms account inaccurate - REMOVAL LIKELY"
  - Shows what it means in real terms

## Lower Section - Knowledge Hub:
- "You asked about hardship letters" → link to module
- "Your oldest negative item ages out in 18 months" → countdown
- "Collections account in SOL window?" → link to tactics
- Education happens INSIDE the work, not separate

## Managed Users See Different View:
- "We sent 3 letters this week on your behalf"
- Timeline of OUR actions (we're accountable)
- "Next: Bureau has 30 days to respond. We'll follow up on [date]"
- Shows PARTNERSHIP, not us doing it TO them

## Psychology:
- Green for wins (removals, disputes resolved)
- Amber for pending (we're waiting for response)
- Shows MOMENTUM not just status
- Every page teaches you something about credit laws/your rights
